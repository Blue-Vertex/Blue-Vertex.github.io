Mathematics intro (swedish)

Chalmers University of Technology and the University of Gothenburg. A two week mathematics intro-course consisting of four lectures for various engineers and science programs (2020). Contents: Sets, logic, functions, sums and series

Discrete mathematics (swedish)

Chalmers University of Technology. A seven week course in discreet mathematics for first year computer science engineering students (2020). Contents: Logic, integer arithmetic, congruences, cryptography, sequences, probability, combinatorics, graphs, relations, binary operations.

Linear algebra (swedish)

Chalmers University of Technology. A seven week course in linear algebra for first year computer science engineering students (2020). Contents: Linear systems, vectors, lines and planes in 3d, inner product, matrices, linear maps, determinants, eigenvalues
